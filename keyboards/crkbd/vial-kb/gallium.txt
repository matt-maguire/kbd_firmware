Gallium Ortho:

`  6  7  8  9  0   1  2  3  4  5  \
   b  l  d  c  v   j  y  o  u  ,  -
   n  r  t  s  g   p  h  a  e  i  ;
   q  x  m  w  z   k  f  '  /  .
        LA SP LC   RS RG RA

Notes:
- LA=Left Alt; SP=Space; LC=Left Ctl
- RS=Right Shift; RG=Right GUI; RA=Right Alt
- Q & X are swapped from standard Gallium; this makes
  it easier to use Emacs on an angle-modded keyboard
  and moves the destructive Q shortcut out of the way.
- swapped / & ; to make question mark more accessible
  on Corne mini 5x3 keyboard.
